Welcome to Dental Connect

We are group of Doctors who are associated together to bring in the best of clinical practice nearer to our patients at the best and affordable price. We partner with the best to dentists to ensure we provide the quality care across our centers..

We understand the importance of the oral-systemic connection and aim to share knowledge with our patients on how a healthy mouth can positively impact the well-being of the entire body. Our mission is to provide effective, and state of the art dental treatment in a welcoming and relaxing environment. We pride ourselves on focusing on prevention, as it is the key to our overall health. We are passionate about dentistry and our energy and positive spirit shine through in our actions and words.

The Team of Doctors at Dental Connect has vast experience and are experts in their respective fields. We implement only research and clinical based techniques and have developed and perfected a system of care that provides long lasting, beautiful results.

We proudly provide comprehensive quality dental care to the society. Our featured services include preventative and restorative (fillings, crowns) treatment, Dental Implants, removable (partials, dentures) appliances, TMJ Dysfunction, Invisalign, and much more.

For us patients come first and we strive to provide them the best of dental care at affordable price.

School Dental Programme: The School Dental Service provides dental services in school dental clinics and mobile dental clinics for Primary and Secondary pupils to promote good oral health .
